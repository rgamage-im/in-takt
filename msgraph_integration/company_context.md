## What We Do
1. Overall Mission
Integral Methods LLC is a mission-driven startup focused on solving the housing affordability crisis through panelized offsite construction. Our core innovation is a fully integrated building system that shifts nearly all value creation from the jobsite to the factory. This includes:
* Structure, insulation, wiring, plumbing, windows, doors, and finishes built into panels.
* Mistake-proof design that minimizes reliance on skilled labor and enables rapid assembly (1–2 days).
* High-density transport that allows fewer factories to serve broader geographies.
We aim to deliver homes that are 60% less expensive, code-compliant, and highly livable, with a long-term goal of producing 100,000 units per year.
* History of our team: Most of our employees came from the same previous company, Schilling Robotics, founded by Tyler Schilling.
